Conference Scope

Each of the topic areas is expanded below but the sub-topics list is not exhaustive. Papers may address one or more of the listed subtopics, although authors should not feel limited by them. Unlisted but related sub-topics are also acceptable, provided they fit in one of the following main topic areas:

  • All fields of Chemical Engineering
  • Alternayive Fuels & Fuel Analysis
  • Waste to energy technology
  • Molecular Biotechnology
  • Molecular Modeling
  • Nanotechnology
  • Bio-material
  • Bioengineering
  • Biodiversity and Environmental Biology
  • Oil & Petroleum
  • Heat & Mass Transfer
  • Process Design
  • Polymer & Plastics Engineering
  • Coal Processing Technology
